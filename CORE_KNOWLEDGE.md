## 2. MATERIAL REPOSITORY
Categorization logic used by the Recommendation Engine and AI Curator.

### Natural Selection
*   **Granite:** Heat resistant, scratch proof. The "Workhorse."
*   **Marble:** Classical beauty. Prone to etching and scratching. High maintenance.
*   **Quartzite:** "The Master Selection." Crystalline beauty with Granite-level hardness.
*   **Onyx:** Translucent and decorative. Requires master handling.

### Engineered Selection
*   **Quartz:** Maintenance-free, non-porous. Indoor use only (UV sensitive).
*   **Dekton:** Ultra-compact. Fire-proof and UV-stable. Perfect for Florida exteriors.
*   **Porcelain:** Lightweight, large-format slabs. Ideal for modern, seamless looks.
